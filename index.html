What is Affiliate Marketing?

You are Probably here because you want to make money online with affiliate marketing or just heard about it or stumbled upon this post randomly.

Now what is affiliate marketing.....

It is simple....you have to promote products/services that are someone else's.You can add special links that is your affiliate links to those products/services in your content or anyother resources you use to drive traffic.When your readers or client or customers click those links or buy or purchase products after clicking those link you get a cut of the profits or you get a part of the profit from the merchant or owner of the product who is selling.Easy enough to understand right.
